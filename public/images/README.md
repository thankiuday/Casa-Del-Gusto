# Images Directory

This directory contains all local images for the Restaurant Theme.

## Structure

- `hero/` - Hero section images for pages
- `menu/` - Menu item food photography  
- `gallery/` - Gallery images
- `blog/` - Blog post images
- `team/` - Team member photos
- `testimonials/` - Customer testimonial photos
- `general/` - Miscellaneous images (map, decorative, etc.)

## Setup

1. See `IMAGE_MAPPING.md` in the root directory for complete download instructions
2. Download all images from Unsplash URLs to their designated folders
3. Or replace with your own professional photography

## Image Requirements

- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Hero images:** Recommended 1920x1080px or higher
- **Menu items:** Recommended 800x800px
- **Gallery:** Recommended 800x600px
- **Team/Testimonials:** Recommended 400x400px (square)
- **Blog:** Recommended 1200x800px

## Important

All placeholder images from Unsplash are for DEMO PURPOSES ONLY. Replace with your own images before launching your website.

For best results, use professional food photography that matches your restaurant's brand and style.
